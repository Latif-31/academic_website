about

I am an Assistant Professor of Statistics and Artificial Intelligence at ESISA. Previously, I served as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Université de Montréal and the University of Ottawa. My primary research focus is on Statistical Machine Learning with applications in Quantitative Finance.

My research contributions include:

Cryptocurrency & Stock Market Forecasting

Developing machine learning and statistical modeling frameworks for cryptocurrency and stock market forecasting.

IoT Security Enhancement

Implementing machine learning algorithms to enhance the security of IoT systems against cyberattacks.

Blockchain Security Analysis

Designing probabilistic models for the security analysis of blockchain systems.
